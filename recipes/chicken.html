Oven Fried Chicken

Description

Indulge in the crunchy delight of Oven-Fried Chicken, a healthier yet equally satisfying twist on the classic fried favorite. This recipe simplifies the process, offering a hassle-free alternative that skips the splatter of oil without missing out on flavor. With a crunchy exterior of seasoned breadcrumbs and a juicy, tender interior, this oven-fried chicken promises a golden-brown finish and a smoky hint of paprika. It's a versatile dish that can be made with your choice of chicken cuts, from thighs to drumsticks. Serve this homestyle comfort on your dinner table in just over an hour, pairing it with your favorite sides for a meal that's sure to please. Leftovers, if any, can be stored and savored for days to come.

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken thighs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste. Put bread crumbs in another shallow plate or bowl and beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs, until all pieces are coated.
  4. Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn pieces over and bake for another 30 minutes. Remove from the oven and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
